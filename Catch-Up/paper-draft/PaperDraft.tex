\begin{abstract}
In Colombia, tons of food are wasted annually. Amid a global food loss and waste crisis (which exacerbates environmental damage and food insecurity), this underscores the urgent need for collaborative digital platforms that reduce coordination barriers between donors and recipients. Therefore, this document proposes a mobile application that connects local cafes and restaurants with students, charities, and citizens in Bogotá through geolocation, real-time notifications, and priority rules for verified organizations. This aims to achieve quantifiable reductions in waste, greater social equity, and alignment with the principles of the circular economy.
\end{abstract}
